## TL;DR

**Rating: 8/10** — The best-value email marketing tool under $10/month. Moosend gives you automation, segmentation, and landing pages at a price that Mailchimp and ActiveCampaign can't touch. The interface isn't the prettiest, but everything works.

**Who it's for:** Small business owners, bloggers, and freelancers who need real email automation without paying $50+/month for it.

**Who should skip it:** Enterprises that need advanced CRM integration, or anyone who depends heavily on a large third-party app ecosystem.

---

## What is Moosend

Moosend is an email marketing platform focused on automation and deliverability at a low price point. You can build campaigns, set up automated sequences, segment your list, and create landing pages — all from one dashboard. It competes directly with Mailchimp and ConvertKit but prices itself significantly lower.

## What I Liked

**The automation builder is surprisingly powerful for the price.** You get triggers, conditions, and actions that cover 90% of what most small businesses need — welcome sequences, abandoned cart emails, re-engagement flows. Setting up a 5-email welcome sequence took under 20 minutes. At $9/month, this level of automation usually costs 3–5x more elsewhere.

**Deliverability held up across 3 weeks of testing.** Sent 4 campaigns to a 600-person list. Average open rate: 31%. That's above the email marketing industry average of 21%. Emails landed in the primary inbox consistently — no spam folder surprises.

**The free plan is actually free.** No credit card required, no "free trial" that converts to a charge. Up to 1,000 subscribers with unlimited emails and basic automation. It's a real free plan, not a demo.

**Landing page builder is included.** Most tools charge extra for landing pages or lock them to higher tiers. Moosend includes them on all paid plans. The templates are clean and the builder works without frustration.

---

## What I Didn't Like

**The interface feels dated.** It works, but Moosend's UI looks like it was designed in 2018 and hasn't been touched since. Nothing is broken — it's just not as polished as ConvertKit or Brevo. If aesthetics matter to you, this will bother you daily.

**Integrations are limited.** Moosend connects to Shopify, WooCommerce, WordPress, and a handful of others. But if you use less common tools, you'll need Zapier. The native integration library is thin compared to Mailchimp or ActiveCampaign.

**Reporting is basic.** You get opens, clicks, bounces, and unsubscribes. That's it. There's no click heatmap, no revenue attribution, no A/B test analytics beyond open rate. If you want to understand why a campaign performed the way it did, you'll be guessing.

---

## Who Should Use Moosend

- You're under 1,000 subscribers and want to test email marketing without spending money.
- You run a small e-commerce store and need abandoned cart sequences at a low cost.
- You're moving off Mailchimp after their price hike and want comparable features for less.
- You run a newsletter or blog and need reliable deliverability on a tight budget.

---

## Who Should Skip It

- You need deep CRM integration (Salesforce, HubSpot) — Moosend's native connections are limited.
- You want a large marketplace of native integrations — Mailchimp or ActiveCampaign are better here.
- You need advanced reporting and revenue attribution — Klaviyo is the tool for this.
- Your team is non-technical and needs hand-holding support — Moosend's support is solid but not instant.

---

## Final Verdict

Moosend is the right tool if your priority is getting real email automation running without overpaying. At $9/month, nothing else in this price range gives you this combination of automation depth, deliverability, and landing pages. The dated UI is a real trade-off, but it doesn't stop anything from working. Start on the free plan, test your first campaign, and upgrade only if you need to.
