## What You'll Build

By the end of this tutorial, you'll have Jasper AI set up with your brand voice and a complete content piece written — from brief to final draft — in under 30 minutes.

## Prerequisites

- A Jasper AI account ([start your 7-day free trial](AFFILIATE_LINK))
- One piece of existing content that represents your brand voice (blog post, email, landing page)
- A topic or keyword you want to write about

## Step 1: Set Up Your Brand Voice

This is the single most important step. Skip it and Jasper produces generic marketing copy. Do it right and the output actually sounds like you.

Go to **Brand Voice** in the left sidebar. Click **Create Brand Voice**. You have two options:

- **Upload text:** Paste 3-5 examples of your best writing (minimum 500 words total)
- **URL scan:** Enter your website and Jasper analyzes your existing content

Pick upload for more control. Paste your best-performing blog post or email — the one that sounds most like "you." Name the voice something memorable ("Blog voice" or "Email voice").

Jasper will analyze tone, sentence structure, vocabulary level, and formality. You'll see a preview — tweak until it matches.

## Step 2: Create a Campaign Brief

Campaigns keep Jasper focused. Instead of re-explaining your product and audience for every piece of content, you tell it once and reuse across assets.

Go to **Campaigns** → **New Campaign**. Fill in:

- **Product/Service:** What you're promoting (one sentence)
- **Target audience:** Who reads this (specific, not "everyone")
- **Key messages:** 2-3 points you always want to hit
- **Tone:** Select your brand voice from Step 1

Example: "SaaS review blog targeting freelancers who want affordable tools. Key messages: honest reviews, budget-friendly picks, no corporate jargon."

## Step 3: Write Your First Blog Post

Go to **Content** → **New Document** → **Blog Post**. Select your campaign and brand voice.

Enter your target keyword or topic. Jasper generates an outline. Review it — move sections around, delete irrelevant ones, add your own headings if needed.

Click **Generate** on each section. Jasper writes 2-3 paragraphs per heading based on your brief and brand voice. The first draft is usually 70-80% usable.

**Pro tip:** Don't generate the entire post at once. Work section by section. After each generation, edit for accuracy before moving on. Jasper builds on previous sections, so fixing errors early prevents them from compounding.

## Step 4: Edit and Refine

The raw output needs your brain. Common edits:

- **Remove filler phrases:** Jasper still defaults to "In today's fast-paced world" sometimes. Delete on sight.
- **Add specific examples:** AI writes generically. Add your real experiences, numbers, or screenshots.
- **Fix facts:** Jasper can hallucinate features or pricing. Verify everything against the actual product.
- **Adjust length:** Sections that ramble? Cut to 2-3 sentences. Sections too thin? Ask Jasper to expand with a specific prompt like "add a comparison to [competitor]."

Budget 10-15 minutes of editing per 1,000 words. The output is a strong first draft, not a final one.

## Step 5: Use the SEO Integration

If you have Surfer SEO connected (Jasper integrates natively), click the **SEO** tab in the document editor. Enter your target keyword.

Jasper shows real-time optimization: keyword density, related terms to include, suggested word count, and heading structure. Adjust your draft to hit a content score above 70.

No Surfer subscription? Skip this step and optimize manually using free tools like Clearscope's free checker or just Google your keyword and match what the top results cover.

## Step 6: Generate Supporting Assets

This is where campaigns shine. From the same brief, generate:

- **Meta description:** Content → SEO → Meta Description. Pick the best of 3 options.
- **Social posts:** Content → Social Media → select platform. Jasper adapts the blog content to tweet-length or LinkedIn format.
- **Email subject lines:** Content → Email → Subject Lines. Generate 10, pick the best 2 for A/B testing.

One blog post becomes 5-6 assets in 10 minutes. This is the real time savings — not the blog draft itself.

## Step 7: Export and Publish

Click **Export** → choose format (Google Doc, Word, HTML, or plain text). Or copy-paste directly into your CMS.

For blog posts, I export as plain text and paste into my Astro MDX file. For emails, I copy directly into Beehiiv's editor.

## Result

You should now have:
- A trained brand voice that makes all future content consistent
- A campaign brief you can reuse for every piece in that category
- A complete blog post (1,000-2,000 words), edited and ready to publish
- Supporting assets (meta description, social posts, email subject lines)

Total time: 25-35 minutes for the first post. Subsequent posts take 15-20 minutes once your voice and campaign are set up.
